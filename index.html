Hello, I’m Vivek Singh

– a B.Tech graduate in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Data Science.

My journey into analytics began with a strong academic foundation and grew through hands-on projects that bridge data with real-world decision-making.

This project automated vendor data tracking, enabling performance comparisons, shipment monitoring, and KPI reporting with a scalable structure. It reflects my ability to combine technical depth with business-driven outcomes.

Alongside these, I gained practical exposure through internships. Later, as a Python Developer Intern, I built a Hybrid Recommendation Engine, which applied collaborative filtering and NLP techniques to personalize user experiences. Both experiences complemented my academic and personal projects, allowing me to blend statistical understanding with hands-on development.

What drives me is the balance of clarity and impact—turning numbers into narratives that businesses can trust for growth. Guided by structured processes yet open to innovation, I approach analytics with discipline and creativity.

I am actively open to roles such as Data Analyst, Power BI Developer, SQL Developer, Business Intelligence Analyst, and Junior Data Scientist, where I can leverage my technical expertise and problem-solving mindset to deliver value through data.
