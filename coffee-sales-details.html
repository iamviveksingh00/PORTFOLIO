Coffee Shop Sales Analysis

A data-driven project using Excel to analyze coffee shop sales trends and provide key business insights.

Problem Statement

Cafés struggle with low profitability due to poor sales tracking and pricing strategies. This project analyzes sales data to identify peak sales hours, best-selling products, and customer preferences for better decision-making.

Solution Approach

  • Data Cleaning & Processing: Organized raw sales data in Excel
  • Analysis & Insights: Used Pivot Tables, Charts, and Conditional Formatting
  • Visualization: Built an interactive dashboard to track trends

Tools Used: Excel (Pivot Tables, Slicers, Charts, Conditional Formatting)

Key Findings & Business Impact

  • Peak Sales Time: 8 AM - 10 AM (highest footfall)
  • Best-Selling Product: Barista Espresso (25% of revenue)
  • Top Performing Store: Hell’s Kitchen ($236,511 revenue)
  • Customer Spend Per Order: $4.69

Business Impact: Helps managers optimize pricing & marketing strategies and identify high-revenue locations.
